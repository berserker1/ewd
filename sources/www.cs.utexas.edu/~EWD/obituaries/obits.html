Obituaries and Memorials
Edsger W. Dijkstra, Professor Emeritus of Computer Sciences and Mathematics at The University of Texas at Austin, died at his home in Nuenen, the Netherlands on 6 August 2002.

The Computer Sciences Department's obituary for him can be found here.

A remembrance of him by the department's chairman can be found here.

The Memorial Resolution written for the The University of Texas is available in two formats: A4 and US Letter.

The Register Newsletter has a short obituary.
